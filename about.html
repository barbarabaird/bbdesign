Barbara Baird




I am a Web Designer from the Austin TX area. I graduated from the University of Maryland University College in December of 2016; with a Bachelor's of Science in Digital Media/ Web Technology. I have been interested in web design for about 10 years, but never really had the chance to learn how to design sites. In 2014 I was given the opportunity to go back to college, I knew immediately what I wanted to study- Web Design. I love the challenge of taking an idea and making a web page from it.

I am a wife and mother of 2. This year my husband and I celebrated our 20th wedding anniversary. We have 2 daughters who keep us very busy. I love arts and crafts; mostly I do rubber-stamping and scrapbooking, but I will try just about anything. I think all of my years of crafting have really helped me as a web designer. As a crafter I have learned to work with color, typography, and arranging images; all of these are important skills to have as a web designer. My husband and I are both very involved at our church. One of my friends and I just started a branch of the Women’s Ministry. Created to Create was born out of our love of crafting and learning how to use that talent to serve others around us. I also serve on the Audio/Video team making sure that all the slides and videos run smoothly for service.
